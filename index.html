About Us

WATANI LLC is a traditional Afghan food and bakery company based in Alexandria, Virginia. We offer fresh bread, Bolani, cakes, cookies, and authentic Afghan specialties like Watai burgers and tea. Our goal is to bring warmth and flavor from Afghanistan to your home.

Our Products

  • Bolani – Spinach, Potato, Pumpkin, Chicken, Beef
  • Fresh Breads – Afghan traditional breads and flatbreads
  • Bakery – Croissants, Cookies, Biscuits
  • Afghan Snacks – Doughnuts, Fried pastries
  • Beverages – Afghan Tea, Coffee
